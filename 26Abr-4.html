26 Abr. 2021

Encounter with a mummy portrait

rendered with haste. nearly caustic. like lye, a grinning likeness—

he ought to be grinning, after time-travel, after empires pass a sunset,

a reduction simmers so dangerously bellow the threshold,

whose pot holder catches flame, burning Pyrex glass

whose gaze grips before it rips before it crushes—

the type of grip that can only happen between parties of two.

anonymous renderer, faltering renderee, too—

round eyes, cowboy stache, comprise deceased’s likeness,

layers proximity onto my last five shameful crushes

at Akbar, on Sunset.

behind eyes, riding front windows, tinted glass,

windows you can’t check anyone out through, form a threshold—

regulars and visitors divided, nervously I face the reverent threshold.

nervous to bring in someone who’s been dead a millennia, maybe two.

I wanna be reciprocated behind glass,

tropes bisecting the reflection that splits our likeness

like two beams in your car mirror at Sunset

as tires grate, and asphalt, under a car’s weight, crushes.

erosion upturns your face, Altoid crushes,

icy, a wind tunnel, a force-field, a threshold

speeding across-the-street, as you wait for the bus on Sunset.

I wonder if the in Rome they ever had to wait for the 2.

Romulus, Remus, probably had a likeness,

but your own forgotten twin’s name stays a myth behind glass.

think of whose job it was to buy all the glass

in the whole of the Getty Villa— the weight of acquisitions crushes

leave indentations from their origin point, a likeness

where you would be if not behind the threshold

separating you and I, seamed in two

sections, UV-proof, cut off forever from a sunset.

say I broke you out of here, we’d be just in time for Sunset.

with Will Rogers not too hard to get to,

closing time— the watchful gaze of the security guard crushes

he might be able to hear the thoughts I’m sending through the glass.

thoughts like a frisbee, shatter the threshold,

was it the frisbee’s dynamism or your likeness?

getaway car, a team of two— hemispheric crushes,

tropic of cancer, your eye’s likeness, tropic of capricorn, a mouth’s threshold.

equator blowing powdered glass (sand) across Sunset.
